Intelligence Calibration

Yahoo Mail Premium
Home Surface Research

Can the AI understand your email life well enough to replace the inbox? Ongoing research with 5–7 dedicated dogfooders from our target audience, using a flexible toolkit of methods to calibrate the intelligence, find where it breaks, and build confidence in what works. Calibration quality directly affects the Concept Gauntlet's Live Data Cohort (users see intelligence on their own ported data, quirks and all), and informs the curated output for the Anonymized Sample Data Cohort (real but anonymized data tuned to aspirational intelligence quality).

7 Key Questions
1
Personal importance vs. generic urgency — does it know what matters to this person?
2
Connecting dots across messages — can it see the renovation, not just 3 separate emails?
3
"Since you were last here" quality — right altitude? Complete but concise?
4
Improvement trajectory — getting better over time, or plateauing?
5
Feels like it knows you — trusted assistant or algorithm?
6
Graceful failure — when it's wrong, is it catastrophic or forgivable?
7
Scales across frequencies — 2 hours away vs. 48 hours, 4 emails vs. 60?
